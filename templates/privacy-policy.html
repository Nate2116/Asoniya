Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your information.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Asoniya ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our travel platform and services.

This policy applies to all users of our platform, including visitors browsing destinations, registered users creating trip plans, and customers making bookings for accommodations, car rentals, and travel agency services in Ethiopia.

Key Commitment: We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly
  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: Travel preferences, accommodation preferences, dietary restrictions
  • Booking Information: Travel dates, destination selections, number of travelers, special requirements
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely by payment providers)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our platform, customer service interactions
  • Reviews and Ratings: Feedback about destinations, accommodations, and travel agencies
2.2 Information Collected Automatically
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on platform, search queries, destination preferences
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • Calendar Interactions: Date selections, trip duration preferences
  • Selection Patterns: Destinations, attractions, and accommodations you view or select
2.3 Information from Third Parties
  • Travel Agency Partners: Booking confirmations, service updates, itinerary changes
  • Accommodation Providers: Reservation details, check-in/check-out information
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations, payment status
  • Social Media: Information if you choose to connect social media accounts

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision
  • Process bookings for accommodations, car rentals, and travel services
  • Facilitate connections with travel agencies and tour operators
  • Provide trip planning tools and destination recommendations
  • Send booking confirmations and travel updates
  • Calculate trip costs and process payments
3.2 Platform Improvement
  • Analyze user behavior to improve website functionality
  • Enhance destination and attraction recommendations
  • Optimize search and filtering capabilities
  • Develop new features based on user preferences
  • Improve mobile responsiveness and user experience
3.3 Communication
  • Send important service announcements and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Share travel tips and destination information (with consent)
  • Send promotional offers for Ethiopian travel experiences (opt-in only)
  • Notify about changes to bookings or travel conditions
3.4 Security and Legal Compliance
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Ensure platform security and user safety
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our terms of service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers and Partners

We share necessary information with:

  • Hotels and Accommodations: Guest details for reservations and check-in
  • Travel Agencies: Contact information and travel preferences for trip planning
  • Car Rental Companies: Driver information and rental details
  • Payment Processors: Financial information for secure transaction processing
  • Tour Operators: Participant information for guided tours and attractions
4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction. Users will be notified of any such changes.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes and government requests
  • Protect the rights and safety of Asoniya, users, and the public
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms of service
  • Respond to claims of illegal activity or infringement
4.4 Aggregated and Anonymous Data

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information about travel trends, popular destinations, and platform usage statistics for research and marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Firewalls: Network security measures to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates applied promptly
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
5.2 Payment Security
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • No storage of complete credit card numbers on our servers
  • Secure payment gateways for all transactions
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems
5.3 Employee Training

All employees receive regular training on data protection, privacy practices, and security protocols.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and user authentication
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and selections
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)
6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality, such as:

  • Saving destination and date selections
  • Maintaining user login sessions
  • Remembering language and display preferences
  • Providing personalized recommendations

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Portability
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • View your booking history and travel preferences
7.2 Correction and Updates
  • Update your profile information through your account settings
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Modify travel preferences and contact details
7.3 Deletion and Restriction
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Restrict processing of your information for specific purposes
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
7.4 Communication Preferences
  • Opt out of promotional emails and newsletters
  • Choose which types of notifications to receive
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active plus 3 years after closure
  • Booking Records: Kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Payment Information: Processed and deleted according to payment provider policies
  • Communication Logs: Retained for 2 years for customer service purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely

9. International Data Transfers

As a platform focused on Ethiopian tourism, most data processing occurs within Ethiopia. However, some services may involve international transfers:

  • Cloud storage providers with global infrastructure
  • International payment processing systems
  • Customer support and communication tools

All international transfers are conducted with appropriate safeguards and comply with applicable data protection laws.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

For travelers under 18, parental or guardian consent is required for bookings and account creation.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users via email for significant changes
  • Provide a prominent notice on our platform when appropriate

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:
