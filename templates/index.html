Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know before your Ethiopian adventure

About Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a land of ancient history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant cultures. It boasts unique tourist attractions such as the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, the Simien Mountains, and the historical city of Axum.

Legal, Medical & Visa Requirements

Visitors must obtain a visa before arrival or on arrival for eligible nationalities. Ensure vaccinations for yellow fever and other necessary health precautions.

Current Exchange Rates

The Ethiopian currency is the Ethiopian Birr (ETB). Check with local banks for the latest exchange rates.

Emergency Information

  • Police: 101
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Fire: 103
  • Embassy: Contact your respective embassy for assistance.
